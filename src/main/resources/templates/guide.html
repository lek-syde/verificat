COVID-19 certificate verification

  • How to scan the QR-CODE on your vaccination card

    The QR-CODE is located on your COVID-19 vaccination card typically at the bottom right on the cover page.
    You can scan the code by using the camera of your mobile device/tablet. For devices that do not support scanning QR code directly from the device camera. You can download a QR-CODE scanner application on the Apple store or google playstore.

    Note: if you are unable to scan your QR-CODE due to physical damage please visit the your vaccination site for QR CODE reissuance.
    please visit the vaccination site where you took the 1st dose or any of the supersites located in all the 36 states and FCT for replacement. The list can be accessed on NPHCDA's website: www.nphcda.gov.ng

  • Incorrect details? Name, DOB, ID Type and ID Number

    1. Click on the “update” button at the top of your e-certificate.
    Note: if you do not see this button at the top of your e-certificate. please contact the call center 0700 220 1122

    2. A popup would appear to enter the phone number/email address [1] you provided during registration.
    Click the “confirm” button [2]

    3. if you enter the correct details. you would be granted access and redirected to your profile page.
    Edit your incorrect details and click ‘save profile’ [1]

  • Incorrect vaccination details? status, batch number ...

    1. Click on the “update” button at the top of your e-certificate.
    Note: if you do not see this button at the top of your e-certificate. please call the NPHCDA COVID-19 call center on 0700 220 1122

    2. A popup would appear to enter the phone number/email address [1] you provided during registration.
    Click the “confirm” button [2]

    3. if you enter the correct details. you would be granted access and redirected to your profile page.

    4. Click on ‘vaccination history’ [1] to view the list of vaccination doses taken



    5. At the button of the page the contact details of a focal representative (s) would be displayed. You can contact the numbers provided to assist in resolving your issue. If you are unable to contact them, please call the NPHCDA COVID-19 call center on 0700 220 1122

  • how do I know I am due for my second dose

    1. A notification will be displayed as soon as you scan your QR code with a link to the list of vaccination sites would be displayed.

  • ERROR: record has not been linked or might not exist

    After scanning your QR code, if a prompt showing record has not been link or might not exist please call the NPHCDA's COVID-19 call center on 0700 220 1122 or the state representative as listed on the portal. please contact the call center. 0700 220 1122
